self closing tag:-
          Self-closing tags, also known as void or empty tags, are HTML tags that do not require a closing tag. 
          They are written with a trailing slash ("/") before the closing angle bracket.
          Examples include <br>, <img>, <input>, and <meta> tags.

meta tags:-
           meta tags are HTML elements placed within the <head> section of a webpage. 
           They provide metadata or additional information about the web page. 
             Meta tags do not have any visible content when rendered in a browser.
             Meta tags can impact search engine optimization (SEO) by providing information to search engines during indexing.
